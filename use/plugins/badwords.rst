
.. _plugin-badwords:

The BadWords plugin
===================

.. command-sub:

sub 
^^^^



.. command-list:

list
^^^^

Returns the list of words being censored.


.. command-remove:

remove <word> [<word> ...]
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Removes *<word>*s from the list of words being censored.


.. command-add:

add <word> [<word> ...]
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Adds all *<word>*s to the list of words being censored.
